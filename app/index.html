Case Study - Willbuilt.co.uk

The idea behind this site was working off the idea of the blueprint. Firstly because I have always had a love of architecture and secondly who doesn't love classic Jay-z :)


My aim was to create a minimal design with a focus on pixel precision that would work well across devices. To achieve this I used a very simple colour scheme of three colours and latest front-end technologies such as CSS Grid and Flexbox to bring the design together and ensure responsiveness. Finally I used CSS animations and Javascript to bring a bit of life to the final product.
